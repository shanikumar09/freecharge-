More Than Just Spending
A Case For Credit Cards

One of the biggest benefits of a credit card is the interest-free period or credit period that makes repayment of your credit card bill that much easier.

Also when you make payments with your credit card, the balance in your savings accounts accrues interest and while you pay back your credit card bill, you would have earned the interest on that amount. As you can see, making your usual payments with a credit card will actually help you save more money!

Great Power, GREATER RESPONSIBILITY
Credit Card Etiquette

While there are many benefits to applying for a credit card, there are also some responsibilities. Using your credit card sensibly is the best way to take full advantage of its perks.

On-time payments

One of the first rules of using a credit card is to pay your credit card bill on time. This is the only way you can reap the benefits of the interest-free period. If you do not pay your credit card bill on time, you will have to pay interest on your payments. If you don’t pay your credit card bills on time, it will also adversely affect your credit score.

PURCHASE EARLIER FOR LONGER CREDIT PERIOD

Credit card payments are based on the billing cycles, it would be better to purchase more expensive items at the start of the billing cycle so you have more time to pay it off.

CHOOSE YOUR CREDIT CARD WISELY

From the credit card eligibility criteria, credit card limit to the minimum spend on your credit card, you need to figure out what are the features you need according to your scope of repayment and income. Getting a credit card that is the right fit for your needs will ensure that your experience using the same will be great.
