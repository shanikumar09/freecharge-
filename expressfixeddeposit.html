Introduction to Fixed Deposits

When it comes to savings, be it for buying a new home, a dream car or for any other need, a Bank Fixed Deposit is a safe investment option. It is always a safe bet to invest in a Fixed Deposit, meaning it will help you grow money over a period of time with zero risk and guaranteed returns. As far as safe investment plans go, Fixed Deposit is the most popular option for savings amongst all financial instruments. In comparison to savings accounts, fixed deposits are safe investments with high returns.

So without further ado, let’s get more details of a Fixed Deposit -

It is a financial instrument that is provided by financial institutions like banks in which you can deposit money for a specific period of time. You earn interest over that period as per the interest rate provided by the institution where you have opened your Fixed Deposit account.

Before we proceed further, we would like to clarify something for our readers. Many people ask us whether an FD is a term deposit. And the answer is that a term deposit, also known as a time deposit, is a deposit that is kept by a bank or financial institution for a specified period prior to which withdrawal of the money (principal + interest) is not possible in the banks whereas Fixed Deposit offered on freecharge app can be withdrawn before maturity. Both the fixed deposit and recurring deposit come under the umbrella of term deposit. Term deposit interest rates are determined by the institution providing the same.

Features of Fixed Deposit

EASILY RENEWABLE

It is very easy to renew a Fixed Deposit in case a depositor does not wish to withdraw their principle and FD interest. With auto-renewal in banks and financial institutions, deposits can be renewed for the same amount of time.

LUMP-SUM INVESTMENT

With the one-time investment feature, you can invest your money in one go and not think of your Fixed Deposit till the time of maturity. So sit back and watch your money grow.

LIQUIDITY

While it is always best to leave a Fixed Deposit until maturity, you can easily redeem a part or full amount of a Fixed Deposit in case of any emergency need.

BENEFITS OF FIXED DEPOSIT

HIGHER FD RATES

The Fixed Deposit interest rates are higher than that of a savings bank account. So rather than just letting the money sit idle in your account, investing in a Fixed Deposit will keep your money safe in the bank and also accrue Fixed Deposit interest.

SAFE & STABLE INVESTMENT

With current bank Fixed Deposit rates, you can easily grow your money. And the best part is that returns are guaranteed, with no loss of principal.

PERIODIC PAYOUTS

If a depositor puts in a large sum of money as FD, they can opt for Fixed Deposit interest to be paid out at regular intervals to manage expenses.

UNAFFECTED BY MARKET FLUCTUATIONS

As previously mentioned, a Fixed Deposit is one of the safest way to invest money and one of the reasons that investors opt for it is due to the fact that market fluctuations don’t affect the maturity payout of an FD in any way.

INCOME TAX ON INTEREST ON FIXED DEPOSIT

Tax on FD interest is applicable, i.e., interest income from a Fixed Deposit is taxable as per the income tax bracket of the investor. If the interest earned by a depositor is less than Rs.10,000 in a year, 10% of TDS will be deducted if PAN details have been submitted to the bank. If PAN details have not been submitted, then 20% TDS will be deducted.
